Total World War


Created by Rolf Larsson and Hepster

AI, Settings and Options



Neutral:Brazil, Spain, Sweden, Turkey only move and produce, but never attack.
Select Does Nothing AI for them.
Set AI Pause Duration to 0 to speed up AI moves(Game->AI Pause Duration-> 0 ms)
Switch off :Confirm Enemy Casualties and Focus on own casualties for more speed,too.
If you want to play without those active neutrals and without the option to attack them, switch off politics.
